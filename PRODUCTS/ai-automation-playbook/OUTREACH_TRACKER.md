## Response Templates (Quick Replies)

**Interested:**
"Great! Let's find 30 minutes to discuss your specific situation. I can show you exactly how AI automation could work for [Company]. Here's my calendar: [link]"

**Questions:**
"Happy to address that. The short answer is: [answer]. But let me give you the full picture on a quick call. When works for you?"

**Not now:**
"Totally understand timing isn't right. Mind if I check back in 3 months? In the meantime, here's a case study you might find interesting: [link]"

**Referral:**
"Thanks for the intro! I'll reach out to [name] and mention you connected us. Appreciate it!"

## Success Patterns

**What's working:**
- Personalized pain point in subject line
- Specific ROI numbers in opening
- Pilot offer (50% off) creates urgency
- Follow-ups get 2x responses of initial outreach

**What's not working:**
- Generic "AI solutions" messaging
- No specific value prop
- Asking for calls without ROI preview
- Too many follow-ups (stop at 3)
