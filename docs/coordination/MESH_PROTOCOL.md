## üö® RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Market Downturn
- **Impact:** Capital deployed loses value
- **Mitigation:** Diversified strategies (40% stable, 40% tactical, 20% moonshots)
- **Backup:** Revenue services generate cash flow regardless of market

### Risk 2: Contributor Disputes
- **Impact:** Conflict over rewards, decisions, contributions
- **Mitigation:** Clear governance protocols, transparent tracking, voting
- **Backup:** Dispute resolution process, ability to fork/exit

### Risk 3: Security Breach
- **Impact:** Hacks, scams, exploits
- **Mitigation:** Non-custodial (everyone holds own funds), audited protocols only
- **Backup:** Insurance (optional), collective fund for losses

### Risk 4: Regulatory Issues
- **Impact:** Government cracks down on collective investing
- **Mitigation:** Not a fund (no pooled capital), optional legal structure
- **Backup:** Dissolve to individual contributors, no legal entity to target

### Risk 5: Failure to 2x
- **Impact:** Some contributors don't hit target
- **Mitigation:** Realistic expectations, diversified strategies, monthly reviews
- **Backup:** Sliding scale (1.5x still good, 1.2x still profit)
