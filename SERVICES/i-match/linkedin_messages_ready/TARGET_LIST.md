### Method 1: LinkedIn Search (Recommended - 30 min)

**Step-by-step:**


#### Search 1: Financial Advisor New York (10 targets)

1. Go to LinkedIn.com and log in
2. Search: "Financial Advisor New York"
3. Click "People" filter
4. Add location filter: "New York, NY"
5. Look for profiles with:
