## Common Questions

### "Isn't this just profit-sharing?"
No. Profit-sharing gives money to employees. Conscious circulation flows resources through an entire ecosystem (spiritual, community, economic) creating expanding cycles of flourishing.

### "Why not just donate profits from a regular company?"
Because structure IS practice. The 3-tier model embodies our values, provides tax efficiency, creates alignment, and demonstrates that commerce itself can be conscious.

### "What if the church misuses funds?"
Trust document and church governance prevent misuse. Community oversight. Transparency. Alignment of incentives. Plus, church status requires mission-focused use of funds.

### "Is this legal?"
Absolutely. 508(c)(1)(a) churches, private trusts, and operating companies are all standard legal entities. The innovation is in how we connect them to create conscious circulation.

### "What about growth vs mission?"
False dichotomy. Sustainable growth FUNDS mission. Without revenue, no mission. Without mission, no alignment, no community, eventually no growth. They're interdependent.
