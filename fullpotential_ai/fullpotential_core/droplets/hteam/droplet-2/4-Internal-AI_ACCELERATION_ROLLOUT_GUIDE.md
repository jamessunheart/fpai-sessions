### "Will AI replace my job?"

**Response:** No - AI amplifies your job. You remain:
- The architect (final decisions)
- The integrator (making it work in our system)
- The deployer (production responsibility)
- The creative (solving novel problems)

AI becomes your 10x force multiplier, not your replacement.

### "What if the AI generates bad code?"

**Response:** That's why we have AI verification (Gemini checks Claude's work) + human deployment (you test before production).

Bad code gets caught in verification phase, not after you've spent time integrating it.

### "This seems too good to be true"

**Response:** We have proof. Droplet #14 is real, working, deployed. The methodology is proven.

Try it on one small feature. Track your time. See for yourself.

### "I don't know how to prompt AI"

**Response:** We provide exact prompts in the quick reference. Copy/paste them. They work.

After 2-3 builds, you'll develop your own style.

### "What if I get stuck?"

- Full guide (detailed troubleshooting)
